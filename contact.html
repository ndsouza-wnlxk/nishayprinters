Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What are your turnaround times?

Turnaround times vary by service: Digital printing (same day), Offset printing (2-3 days), Screen printing (1-2 days), ID cards (same day), Binding (same day). Rush orders available.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! We have professional graphic designers who can help create or refine your designs. We work with print-ready files or can design from scratch.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, PSD, CDR, and high-resolution JPG/PNG files. For best results, please provide print-ready PDF files with 300 DPI resolution and 3mm bleed.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery services within Bengaluru. Delivery charges vary based on location and order size. Free delivery available for large orders.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards. Corporate accounts available for regular customers.
